All Autonomous Driving Mechatronics & Robotics Research Internships Leadership
  • Autonomous Food Pickup/Delivery using a Turtlebot

    We developed a ROS package for a TurtleBot3 Burger to autonomously explore a mock environment where it needed to "pick up and deliver" food items based on the images it saw along the way. This project was a part of the course AA 274 (Principles of Robotic Autonomy) at Stanford

  • Autonomous Driving in a Volkswagen Golf GTI

    We developed controllers for a Volkswagen Golf GTI to autonomously drive around a parking lot in an oval path while ensuring a comfortable ride. This project was a part of the course ME 227 (Vehicle Dynamics and Controls)

  • ME 218A: Smart Product Design Fundamentals

    We designed and built an interactive bike game by integrating the TIVA LaunchPad with different electronic components, encapsulated in a clean mechanical design.

  • ME 218B: Smart Product Design Applications

    We designed and built an autonomous ball collection and depositing robot by integrating the TIVA LaunchPad with different electronic components for sensing and actuation, encapsulated in a clean mechanical design.

  • ME 218C: Smart Product Design Practice

    We designed and built an autonomous hovercraft and controller by integrating the TIVA LaunchPad with different electronic components for sensing and actuation, encapsulated in a clean mechanical design.

  • Autonomous Ball Picking Robot (Capstone Design Project)

    In this project, we designed an Autonomous Ball Collecting and Depositing Robot with me as the Controls and Design Lead of this Project

  • AICHE Chem-E-Car Competition 2017
